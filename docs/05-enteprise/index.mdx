# Enterprise Features

Escape's advanced enterprise suite is tailored to meet the requirements of modern organizations. Built with scalability, security, and ease-of-use in mind, these features ensure that businesses, regardless of size, can use Escape confidently and effectively.

## Features Overview:

- **SSO / Identity Federation / SAML**: Seamlessly integrate with your enterprise's Single Sign-On (SSO) infrastructure. Our support for Identity Federation and Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML) ensures swift and secure user authentication.

- **RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)**: Gain granular control over who can do what within Escape. Define roles and assign permissions based on job functions to ensure that users only access what they need.

- **Audit Logs**: Maintain transparency and oversight with comprehensive audit trails. Track changes, access, and operations to support compliance and security initiatives.

- **Private Tenant**: Your dedicated, isolated instance ensures data segregation and enhanced security.

- **Rotating Encryption**: Enhance data security with encryption strategies that periodically rotate encryption keys, making your data even more resilient against threats.

- **Deployment Options with Repeater**: Flexibly deploy Escape in diverse environments. Whether on-premises, in the cloud, or hybrid setups, our Repeater facilitates seamless integration.

- **SLA (Service Level Agreement)**: We're committed to delivering the highest quality of service, backed by our robust Service Level Agreement. Our commitment ensures that your operations run smoothly and consistently.

- **Support**: A dedicated support team is always on standby. From technical issues to usage queries, we're here to assist you every step of the way.

Dive deeper into each feature to understand how they can enhance your organization's security posture and streamline your operations.
