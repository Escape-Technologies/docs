## Supported Compliance Standards

Below are the compliance standards supported by Escape, with brief descriptions and links to official documentation:

- [**OWASP TOP 10**](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/): A standard awareness document representing a broad consensus about the most critical web application security risks.

- [**CWE**](https://cwe.mitre.org/): Common Weakness Enumeration, a community project that identifies common software security weaknesses.

- [**WASC**](http://www.webappsec.org/): Web Application Security Consortium, an organization that produces open source and widely agreed upon best-practice security standards.

- [**PCI-DSS**](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/pci_security/): Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard, a standard for organizations that handle branded credit cards to protect cardholder data.

- [**MITRE ATT&CK**](https://attack.mitre.org/) (Coming Soon): Globally-accessible knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques based on real-world observations. 

- [**HIPPA**](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/) (Coming Soon): Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act, which sets the standard for protecting sensitive patient data.

- [**GDPR**](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj) (Coming Soon): General Data Protection Regulation, a regulation that requires businesses to protect the personal data and privacy of EU citizens.

- [**SOC-2**](https://www.aicpa.org/interestareas/frc/assuranceadvisoryservices/aicpasoc2report.html) (Coming Soon): A framework that defines criteria for managing customer data based on five "trust service principles."

- [**PSD-2**](https://ec.europa.eu/info/law/payment-services-psd-2-directive-eu-2015-2366_en) (Coming Soon): Revised Payment Service Directive, an EU directive to regulate payment services and payment service providers.

- [**ISO27001**](https://www.iso.org/isoiec-27001-information-security.html) (Coming Soon): An international standard on how to manage information security.

- [**HDS**](https://esante.gouv.fr/en/actors/digital-health/hosting-health-data) (Coming Soon): Health Data Hosting, a French standard for hosting providers that store health data.

- [**NIST Framework**](https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework) (Coming Soon): Developed by the U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology, this framework provides guidelines and best practices for managing cybersecurity risk.

- [**HITRUST CSF**](https://hitrustalliance.net/product-tool/hitrust-csf/) (Coming Soon): A certifiable framework that provides organizations with a comprehensive, flexible, and efficient approach to regulatory compliance and risk management.

- [**FedRAMP**](https://www.fedramp.gov) (Coming Soon):  The Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program is a United States federal government-wide compliance program that provides a standardized approach to security assessment, authorization, and continuous monitoring for cloud products and services.

- **...and many more on the horizon.**

Staying compliant is an ongoing process, not a one-time task. With Escape's Compliance feature, you're not only equipped with the tools to validate current security postures but also prepared for future regulatory challenges.
